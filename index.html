What the poster shows

Four steps: race the gates, prove each move safe, break it with a hidden load, then teach the check what it is missing. Tap a card to jump the simulator to that moment.

All results are in simulation on the full nonlinear drone. The safety guarantee is conditional on the sets we check, not absolute. Payload presets are deliberately heavy and provisional. The task-by-task build is mine; the broader active-learning vision is the lab's direction.
