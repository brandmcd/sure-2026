Safe Drone Racing With an Unknown Payload

Brandon McDonald · Kaleb Ben Naveed · Dimitra Panagou

A delivery drone has to fly fast and stay inside a safe zone it can prove it will not leave, even when it is carrying something it has never felt before. Every run below is real, recorded from the simulator.

These are real recorded runs from the gatekeeper simulator: a bare drone that holds the safe zone, and the same drone carrying an unknown swinging load the safety check is blind to, so it leaves the zone. Everything is in simulation; the safe zone is the region the check keeps the drone inside, and the loads are deliberately heavy. Teaching the check to feel the load, so it stays both safe and fast, is the next step. Zipline and Wing already lower packages on a cable like this.
